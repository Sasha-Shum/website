About Me

My name is Aliaksandr Shumski; I am a fourth year student in RIT (Rochester Institute of Technology) majoring Game Design & Development and minoring Criminal Justice. I also got my associate degree in Computer Science. Since childhood, I have been always interested in games and new technologies. Therefore, after getting my degree in Computer Science, I decided to explore a new area in which I was always interested: Games. I do like the idea of creating stunning and unforgettable experience from nothing. That feeling, when people are looking at your work and saying “That looks amazing!” When I am working on any of my projects, I am always trying to go above & beyond in order to learn new skills. After finishing my degree, I am eager to enter the challenging Game Development industry and use my skills to push games to the next generation.
